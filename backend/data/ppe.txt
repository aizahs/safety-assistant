Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

Workers performing grinding, cutting, or welding operations must wear appropriate PPE.
This includes safety glasses with side shields or face shields to protect against flying debris.

Hearing protection such as earplugs or earmuffs is required when operating loud equipment,
including grinders, jackhammers, and power saws.

Hand protection must be worn when handling sharp materials, hot surfaces, or chemicals.
Suitable gloves should be selected based on the hazard involved.

Respiratory protection may be required when cutting concrete, masonry, or other materials
that generate dust. Dust masks or respirators must be used in accordance with safety guidelines.

All PPE must be inspected before use and replaced if damaged.
